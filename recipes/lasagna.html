Lasagna Recipe


Ingredients

  • Dried Lasgna Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mushrooms - Diced
  • Cashew Mozerrella

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil and add lasagna.
  2. once tender, remove pasta from pot and place into cold water.
  3. In a saucepan, prepare tomato sause and diced mushrooms. Season to taste.
  4. Layer pasta sauce and chease in a casserole dish. Repeat until casserole dish is full.
  5. Heat in oven at 425° F for 1 hour.
  6. Remove and cool to serve.
